My First Few Years

I was born on August 25, 2002 In Elmhurst, Illinois, a suburb of Chicago. About 1 and a half years later, my family and I moved to the city, and I have lived in that same house since then. I attended pre school at Lincoln Park Pre School. At age 6, I began attending Alexander Graham Bell Elementary School, in Kindergarten. I started playing organized baseball when I was about 5 years old, and baseball has become a huge part of my life.
